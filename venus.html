VOLCANOES

Venus has numerous volcanoes, with over 1,600 major volcanoes and numerous smaller volcanic features.

  • Venus's volcanoes are relatively young, indicating that the planet has experienced volcanic activity in the recent past.
  • The volcanoes on Venus are thought to be the result of a combination of tectonic activity and volcanic processes.
  • The volcanoes on Venus are relatively small compared to those on other planets.
