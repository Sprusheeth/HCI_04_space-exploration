Planets of Our Solar System

Discover the wonders of each planet, from Mercury to Neptune. Explore their unique features and mysteries.

Mercury Venus Earth Mars Jupiter Saturn Uranus Neptune

Mercury

The closest planet to the Sun, Mercury is a rocky world with extreme temperatures and a cratered surface.

Venus

Known as Earth's twin, Venus has a dense atmosphere of carbon dioxide and surface temperatures hot enough to melt lead.

Earth

The only planet known to support life, Earth is a beautiful blue marble teeming with biodiversity.

Mars

The Red Planet is a cold, dusty desert world with a history of water and potential for future exploration.

Jupiter

The largest planet in our solar system, Jupiter is known for its Great Red Spot, a massive storm.

Saturn

Famous for its stunning ring system, Saturn is a gas giant with numerous moons.

Uranus

An ice giant, Uranus rotates on its side and has a bluish color due to methane in its atmosphere.

Neptune

The farthest planet from the Sun, Neptune is a windy, icy giant with a deep blue hue.
